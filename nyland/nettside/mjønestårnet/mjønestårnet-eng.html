Mjøsatårnet

AB Invest AS and HENT AS have entered into a total contract for the construction of Mjøstårnet in Brumunddal. It will be the world's tallest wooden house.

Mjøstårnet has a total area of approximately 15,000 square meters, extends over 18 floors and includes apartments, hotels, offices, restaurants and associated public areas. Total height is over 80 meters.

Builder: AB Invest AS
                 Years: 2017 - 2019
                 Contract: Interaction / Total Contract
                 Scope: 15,000 sqm
                 Architect: Voll Architects
                 Location: Brumunddal
